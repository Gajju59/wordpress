Why Speech?

Software should not kill clinician productivity. The COVID-19 pandemic is driving several public and private hospitals across Africa to adopt digitized healthcare. Sadly, digitization is creating its own problem. On top of an already daunting job seeing 30+ patients per day, clinicians are spending up to 3x time seeing each patient because typing speed is far from ideal across the board. At many hospitals, clinicians cannot cope with the additional workload and eventually abandon the transition or settle for a hybrid approach. Software should not kill clinician productivity. Clinical speech transcription is ubiquitous across the developing world but virtually absent in Africa. Medical directors across Africa are looking for a solution that specifically boosts clinician adoption and thats where we come in.
